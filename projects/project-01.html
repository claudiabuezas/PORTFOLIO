Castil

(2025)

Peloteo Studio partnered with Castil, a renowned Italian manufacturer of metal seating and outdoor furniture, to deliver a comprehensive rebranding that authentically represents the brand's heritage and forward-thinking vision. Castil's identity is rooted in the fusion of Italian craftsmanship with contemporary design, demanding a visual system that could embody both the strength of industrial steel-bending and the creative versatility of its customizable products.
