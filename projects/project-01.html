Fello

(2025)

Fello's refresh stays close to what made the brand trusted in the first place. In a category full of noise, the identity focuses on clarity, honesty, and ease, reinforcing Fello's no nonsense approach to mobile services. Peloteo introduces a hand drawn speech bubble mascot that becomes both character and structure. It guides the eye, carries messages, and brings warmth to pricing and plans without overcomplicating them. The result is a system that feels friendly, recognisable, and distinctly Swedish, proving that simplicity can still have personality.
